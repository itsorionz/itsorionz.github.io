Ontrack Health - LMS

  • Description: Ontrack Health - Learning Management System (LMS) is a centralized digital platform designed to create, manage, deliver, and track learning content while also providing automated certification upon course or assessment completion. It enables organizations to develop structured courses, manage learning materials, assessments, and issue downloadable or verifiable certificates to learners.

    The system supports content authoring, multimedia integration, version control, and role-based access, ensuring scalable and consistent learning delivery. Built-in analytics and reporting allow administrators to monitor learner progress, performance, and certification status.

    By combining learning management with certificate generation and tracking, the LCMS helps organizations ensure compliance, skill validation, and professional development, making it suitable for corporate training, academic institutions, healthcare education, and professional certification programs.

Key Feature:

  • Candidate Management – Manage learner profiles, enrollment status, progress tracking, assessments, and certification history.
  • Course Management – Create, organize, and publish courses with structured content, assessments, and completion criteria.
  • Instructor Management – Enable instructors to manage course content, track learner performance, and provide feedback.
  • Representative Management – Support organizational or client representatives to monitor assigned candidates and training outcomes.
  • Office Admin Panel – Centralized administrative control for user management, system configuration, reporting, and compliance.
  • Customer Service – Handle learner and client support requests, inquiries, and issue resolution efficiently.
  • Video Library – Central repository for training videos with secure access, categorization, and streaming support.
  • Email Library – Predefined and customizable email templates for notifications, course updates, reminders, and certifications.
